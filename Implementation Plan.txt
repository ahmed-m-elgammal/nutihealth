3.2 Onboarding Data ProcessingCalculation Functions (src/utils/calculations.ts):calculateBMR(weight_kg, height_cm, age, gender):
  - Mifflin-St Jeor Equation
  - Returns basal metabolic rate

calculateTDEE(bmr, activity_level):
  - Multiplies BMR by activity factor
  - Returns total daily energy expenditure

calculateMacros(tdee, goal_type):
  - Protein: 1.6-2.2g per kg bodyweight (higher for muscle gain)
